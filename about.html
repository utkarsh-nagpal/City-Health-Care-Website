About Us



Our Mission:

Advancing Health Together. As a world-class academic and health care system, city Health care strives to transform medicine and health locally and globally through innovative scientific research, rapid translation of breakthrough discoveries, educating future clinical and scientific leaders, advocating and practicing evidence-based medicine to improve community health, and leading efforts to eliminate health inequalities.


We believe that caring can't be taught, kindness isn't a subject learned in medical school, and compassion doesn't come with a degree; these intrinsic qualities are deeply rooted in our culture, which can be seen, heard, and felt throughout our system. We're big enough to provide exceptional care, yet small enough to keep it personalized; we provide healthcare that actually cares.
